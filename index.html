New in Battle for Azeroth

Mythic plus will launch three weeks after Battle for Azeroth is released (September 4th). Here are a few new changes that are coming to mythic plus dungeons in Battle for Azeroth:

      Affixes:
    • Tyrannical and Fortified changes: Tyrannical and Fortified are now keystone level 2 affixes meaning one will always appear on a keystone.
    • New affixes: In addition to other new affixes, Battle for Azeroth is introducing "seasonal affixes", a fourth affix that will be applied to a keystone for the duration of a mythic plus season. The first seasonal affix that is live on the BfA beta is called Infested. One other new affix called Relentless has been datamined, but has not been seen in game yet.

      Gear restrictions:
    • You are now unable to swap gear after a mythic plus dungeon has begun (once the timer has started).

      Threat changes:
    • Threat generation from tanks is now greatly reduced - and some new mobs will outright drop threat.

      Weekly chest loot adjustment:
    • The weekly chest can now contain up to 3 pieces of loot, including one piece of Azerite armor. It will also contain Azerite (aka Battle for Azeroth artifact power).

What is an "affix"?

An affix is a modifier applied to a mythic keystone at certain levels. As of Legion, those levels are 4, 7, and 10. One affix from each pool is applied each week on a set schedule.

Timers

  • GottaGoFast - All-in-one mythic+ timer. Shows % value of mobs on tooltip, timers required for +2/+3 chest and times that objectives are completed.

  • Effective Health - Magic and Physical - Very useful. It shows you how much physical and magic damage you can take. Based on current health, absorbs, and defensive cooldowns from yourself and others.

Keystones

Complete a Mythic Keystone run within the timer to upgrade a keystone.

Complete it with 40% of the timer remaining and upgrade it 3 times in one go. i.e. Keystone level 5 to keystone level 8.

Complete a run with 20% of the timer remaining and upgrade it 2 times in one go. i.e. keystone level 5 to keystone level 7.

Didn't make the timer? That's ok. Your keystone doesn't deplete anymore, it just levels down by 1 and randomizes the dungeon. i.e. keystone level 5 for Blackrook Hold to keystone level 4 Halls of Valor.

Don't have a keystone? Running a mythic plus dungeon will net you a key at the end of the run. You can also get a key from any regular mythic dungeon. This keystone will be one level under the dungeon you complete.

Example: you complete a Halls of Valor +16 - the chest at the end will have a random +15 key in it.

The chest at the end of the run guarantees the GROUP two pieces of loot from the dungeon loot table. If you beat the timer you will be awarded one extra piece of loot.

In addition, roughly every 3 difficulty levels past 15 will award you with an additional piece of loot. (after 15 it's an additive 40% chance per key level for extra loot. +16 has a 40% chance of extra 1 item, +18 has a 100% chance for an extra item (20% chance for two) and +20 has guaranteed 2 extra items for 5 items total.

Example time! You beat a level 15 keystone within the timer and your group is awarded 3 pieces of loot. Later, you beat a level 18 keystone within the timer and your group SHOULD BE awarded with 4 pieces of loot. Repeat every 2 levels. 20 = 5 pieces of loot, 22 = 6 pieces of loot, etc. This is subject to change come Battle for Azeroth.

At the start of a week (after raid/mythic reset) a chest will appear in your order hall giving loot based on the highest level keystone dungeon COMPLETED the previous week, within the timer or not.

In addition, the chest will also have a downgraded keystone based on the highest level keystone run YOU COMPLETED in the previous week.

It is most beneficial to complete a +15 weekly to get the highest item level gear every reset. If that isn't feasible go for the next highest you can complete. Again, you don't need to finish in the timer to get the weekly chest.


Battle for Azeroth notes: the weekly chest can now contain up to 3 pieces of loot, including one piece of Azerite armor. It will also contain Azerite (aka Battle for Azeroth artifact power).

*It will be most beneficial to complete a +10 every week to get the highest gear value from your weekly chest.
